Shipping great work is a discipline. It is a series of small decisions that
compound into something that feels inevitable.

## Make constraints visible

When teams see the constraints clearly, they move with confidence. When they do
not, they over-design and under-ship.

## Craft is a habit

Craft is not a single moment. It is a habit built into every handoff and every
release.
